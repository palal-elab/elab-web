About Us

E Lab Bangladesh Limited

Founded as ERROR Lab in 2014 and rebranded to E Lab Bangladesh Ltd. in 2019, this technology-centric research and development facility has swiftly carved out a niche as the go-to 3rd party R&D service contractor and consultant in Bangladesh. With a strong focus on IoT product development, mass manufacturing, rapid prototyping, 3D modeling and printing, as well as dedicated after-sales services, E Lab offers a comprehensive suite of solutions. Backed by an ensemble of vibrant and innovative Bangladeshi professionals, the facility prides itself on its proficiency in both software and hardware sectors, providing unparalleled industry-grade R&D insights. This steadfast commitment to engineering excellence ensures the seamless transformation of ideas into realities. Open to collaboration with business incubators, government entities, private organizations, or passionate individuals, E Lab is the definitive destination for those in pursuit of avant-garde R&D consultancy and support.
